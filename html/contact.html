Contact FreePI

If you have any questions or feedback about FreePI, we'd love to hear from you!

You can chat with us on the #freepi channel on irc.libera.chat. This is a great way to get quick answers to your questions or to chat with other members of the FreePI community.

We value your feedback and are committed to making FreePI the best package index for discovering and sharing free software. Thank you for your interest in FreePI, and we look forward to hearing from you!
