About FreePI

Welcome to FreePI, a package index that only includes and proposes free software. Our mission is to provide an index for people to share and discover software that respects users' freedoms.

At FreePI, we believe that nonfree software is unethical. Nonfree software is software that doesn't respect users' freedoms. It typically restricts users' ability to use, study, share, and modify the software in some way. This means that nonfree software can be used to control and restrict users, rather than empowering them.

We believe that users have the right to control their computing, and that nonfree software takes away that control. When you use nonfree software, you're effectively giving up control of your computer to the developers of the software.

We believe that free software is essential to maintaining fair and just society. By using FreePI, you can be sure that you're only using and promoting free software, and you can be confident that your computer is under your control.

Thank you for using FreePI, and we look forward to working with you to promote and support free software!
